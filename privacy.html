Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

We collect the following types of information:

Personal Information:

  • Email address (for account creation and communication)
  • Username (chosen by you for platform identification)
  • Age verification data (to ensure eligibility)

Usage Data:

  • Quiz participation history
  • Points and reward balances
  • Challenge completion statistics
  • Device and browser information
  • IP address for security purposes

This data is collected to provide and improve our services, ensure platform security, and deliver rewards.

We use your data for the following purposes:

Service Delivery:

  • Account management and authentication
  • Challenge participation tracking
  • Reward distribution and redemption
  • Performance analytics and improvements

Communication:

  • Platform updates and announcements
  • Reward notifications
  • Support responses
  • Security alerts

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

We use cookies and similar technologies:

Essential Cookies:

  • Session management
  • Security features
  • Preference storage

Analytics Cookies:

  • Usage patterns analysis
  • Platform performance monitoring
  • User experience optimization

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit some platform functionality.

We work with trusted third-party services:

Analytics Providers:

  • Google Analytics (usage tracking)
  • Performance monitoring tools

Reward Partners:

  • Gift card providers
  • Payment processors for wallet credits

Security Services:

  • Fraud detection systems
  • DDoS protection

All third-party services are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and GDPR compliance requirements.

We implement industry-standard security measures:

Technical Safeguards:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Encrypted data storage
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication

Organizational Measures:

  • Staff training on data protection
  • Limited access to personal data
  • Incident response procedures
  • Regular backup systems

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Under data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request data deletion
  • Object to data processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact our support team. We will respond within 30 days.

We retain your data for:

Active Accounts:

  • For the duration of account activity
  • Plus reasonable retention period for legal compliance

Deleted Accounts:

  • Immediate removal of personal identifiers
  • Anonymized data may be retained for analytics
  • Backup systems purged within 90 days

We may retain data longer if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention).

We are committed to protecting children's privacy:

  • Minimum age requirement: 13 years
  • Parental consent required for users under 16 (where applicable)
  • Limited data collection for minor accounts
  • No targeted advertising to children

If we discover data collection from a child without proper consent, we will delete that information immediately.

Questions About Privacy?

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us.
